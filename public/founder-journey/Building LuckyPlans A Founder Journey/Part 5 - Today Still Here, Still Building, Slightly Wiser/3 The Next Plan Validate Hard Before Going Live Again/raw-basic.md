Time period

July 2026 – August 2026 plan

Main content

This blog should explain where LuckyPlans is now and what comes next.

As of July 2026, your current focus is finding real edges for copy-trading parameters again, but with a very different mindset from before.

You are not chasing one beautiful backtest.

You are not rushing because the graph looks exciting.

You are not treating technical readiness as trading validation.

Now the process is more careful:

Find candidate parameters
Run backtests
Verify across different windows
Check fees, spread, slippage, and execution assumptions
Compare simulation behavior
Avoid overfitted results
Only consider real-money execution after enough evidence

You want to validate carefully during July and, if the evidence is strong enough, deposit funds again in August 2026 to execute the strategy.

This should not sound like hype.

It should sound like disciplined confidence.

The key contrast is:

Before, real execution was used to prove the dream.
Now, real execution should only happen after the strategy survives enough tests.

You can also introduce the bigger future direction:

After validating copy-trading parameters, LuckyPlans should expand into strategy research and validation infrastructure.

That means the long-term product is no longer only a copy trading bot. It becomes a broader trading intelligence system:

Leaderboards
Simulation
Trader behavior analysis
Strategy research
Backtesting
Validation infrastructure
Safer copy-trading experiments
More evidence before capital

Core lesson

The next LuckyPlans is not about moving slower.

It is about moving with stronger validation.

“I am still here, still building, but I am not the same founder who saw one beautiful graph and wanted to fly. This time, the plan is simpler: validate harder, execute later, and let the data earn my confidence before I give it my money.”
